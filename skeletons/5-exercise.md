# Session 5 (Exercise)

## Title

Session 5 - Help-Seeking and Building Your Support Network (Exercise)

## Summary

You will learn how to seek support early and effectively instead of waiting for a crisis. This module helps you map your campus network and practice clear communication when asking for help.

## Content

- Why students delay help-seeking and how to break the pattern
- Academic support options: tutoring, office hours, advising
- Social and wellbeing support options on campus
- Communication scripts for asking for help confidently

## Activities

- Build your personal support map (people, offices, channels)
- Draft and practice two help-seeking messages (email/in-person)
- Create a trigger-based action plan for when support is needed

## References & Sources

- Karabenick and Newman, help-seeking in education
- Campus student services documentation
