# Session 8 (Lecture)

## Title

Session 8 - Financial Literacy for College Success (Lecture)

## Summary

This module gives you the money basics that directly affect college outcomes. You will learn how to budget, understand aid and debt decisions, and plan financially for academic continuity.

## Content

- Budgeting foundations for college life
- Financial aid basics and renewal requirements
- Student loans: terms, risks, and decision points
- Cost planning and financial stress prevention

## Activities

- Build a realistic monthly student budget
- Analyze a sample aid package and identify tradeoffs
- Create a personal financial action checklist for next term

## References & Sources

- Federal Student Aid guidance
- Consumer Financial Protection Bureau student tools
- Course budgeting templates and self-check quiz
