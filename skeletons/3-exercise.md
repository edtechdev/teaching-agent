# Session 3 (Exercise)

## Title

Session 3 - Note-Taking and Study Strategies That Actually Work (Exercise)

## Summary

In this module, you will compare passive study habits with methods that improve retention and exam performance. You will practice note-taking and retrieval techniques you can use immediately in your current classes.

## Content

- Effective note-taking approaches (Cornell, mapping, structured summaries)
- Retrieval practice and spaced repetition in real schedules
- Interleaving and elaboration for deeper understanding
- Building a repeatable pre-exam study workflow

## Activities

- Convert one set of class notes into a high-value study guide
- Create a 2-week spaced-repetition plan for one course
- Practice retrieval with a short self-test cycle

## References & Sources

- Dunlosky et al. (2013), effective learning techniques
- Agarwal and Bain (2019), *Powerful Teaching*
- Course examples and self-check quiz
