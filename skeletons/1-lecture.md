# Session 1 (Lecture)

## Title

Session 1 - How Learning Works and Why Mindset Matters (Lecture)

## Summary

In this opening module, you will unpack how learning actually works and why mindset shapes your academic outcomes. You will connect learning science concepts to your own habits and identify practical mindset shifts that help you study smarter.

## Content

- How memory, attention, and retrieval work in real learning
- Common study myths vs. evidence-based strategies
- Growth mindset and academic persistence
- Turning learning science into daily study choices

## Activities

- Reflect on one study habit that is wasting your time and redesign it
- Complete a short retrieval-practice activity
- Draft a personal mindset statement for this semester

## References & Sources

- Brown, Roediger, and McDaniel (2014), *Make It Stick*
- Dweck (2006), *Mindset*
- Course notes and self-check quiz resources
