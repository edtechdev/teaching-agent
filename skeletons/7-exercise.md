# Session 7 (Exercise)

## Title

Session 7 - Career Exploration, Major Choice, and Academic Planning (Exercise)

## Summary

In this module, you will translate interests into direction. You will explore career pathways, compare major options, and draft an academic plan that aligns with your goals and constraints.

## Content

- Career exploration tools and information sources
- Comparing majors: outcomes, fit, and requirements
- Building an academic roadmap by term
- Decision checkpoints and adjustment strategies

## Activities

- Complete a career-interest exploration worksheet
- Compare two potential majors using a decision matrix
- Draft a one-year academic plan with key milestones

## References & Sources

- Career development frameworks (Holland/RIASEC)
- Institution degree-planning resources
- Course planning templates and self-check quiz
