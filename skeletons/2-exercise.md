# Session 2 (Exercise)

## Title

Session 2 - Time Management and Focus Systems (Exercise)

## Summary

This module helps you build a realistic time system you can actually maintain. You will map your current week, identify where time is leaking, and create a focus routine that reduces last-minute stress.

## Content

- Time-audit basics: where your hours currently go
- Planning methods for coursework and deadlines
- Focus systems: deep work blocks, breaks, and distraction control
- Weekly reset and adjustment routines

## Activities

- Build a weekly schedule with fixed and flexible blocks
- Identify your top three distraction triggers and countermeasures
- Run a one-week focus experiment and evaluate results

## References & Sources

- Newport (2016), *Deep Work*
- Claessens et al. (2007), time management and academic outcomes
- Course planning template and self-check quiz
