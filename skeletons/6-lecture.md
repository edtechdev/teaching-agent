# Session 6 (Lecture)

## Title

Session 6 - Values, Purpose, and Goal Setting (Lecture)

## Summary

This module helps you connect day-to-day effort with long-term meaning. You will clarify your values and strengths, then use them to set goals that are specific, realistic, and motivating.

## Content

- Values clarification and decision alignment
- Strengths awareness and growth opportunities
- Goal-setting frameworks for academic progress
- Turning long-term vision into short-term action

## Activities

- Complete a values-prioritization exercise
- Write one semester goal and three milestone actions
- Evaluate goal quality and revise for clarity

## References & Sources

- Locke and Latham, goal-setting theory
- Seligman and Peterson, strengths-based approaches
- Course reflection prompts and self-check quiz
