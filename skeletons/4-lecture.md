# Session 4 (Lecture)

## Title

Session 4 - Stress, Resilience, and Healthy Performance (Lecture)

## Summary

This module focuses on sustainable performance, not burnout. You will learn how stress affects learning and build practical routines for emotional regulation, recovery, and motivation.

## Content

- Stress responses and cognitive performance
- Emotional regulation basics for students
- Motivation systems: identity, goals, and consistency
- Sleep, movement, and recovery habits that support learning

## Activities

- Map your stress signals and early warning signs
- Build a personal reset protocol for high-stress weeks
- Choose one wellbeing habit to implement for 14 days

## References & Sources

- Yeager and Dweck, stress and mindset research
- American College Health Association reports
- Course resilience tools and self-check quiz
