# 📚 College Success Course Bundle

## START HERE

🎓 **Welcome to the complete College Success course package!**

This folder contains everything you need to deliver a comprehensive 16-week asynchronous college success course to your students.

### What's Inside?

- **Complete Course Materials** (16 modules in `/materials/`)
- **Planning Documents** (outline, didactics, agenda in `/docs/`)
- **Validation Report** (quality assurance in `/docs/`)
- **This Bundle** (comprehensive guide and organization)

### How to Get Started (3 Steps)

#### Step 1: Read the Bundle Index
📖 Open **[`BUNDLE-INDEX.md`](./BUNDLE-INDEX.md)** — This is your complete guide to the course

**Time needed:** 20-30 minutes for full read

#### Step 2: Customize for Your Institution  
🏫 Before putting this in your LMS, customize:
- Campus-specific support resources (counseling, tutoring, financial aid, career services)
- Add your institution's name and links
- Update module resources sections with YOUR campus info
- Update deadline dates for YOUR calendar

**Time needed:** 2-4 hours depending on how thorough you are

#### Step 3: Load Into Your LMS
💻 Set up your course:
- Create week/module folders matching the agenda
- Copy materials into your LMS
- Set up discussion forums
- Create assignment submissions for projects
- Add your campus resources

**Time needed:** 4-6 hours for first setup

## What You'll Get

### ✅ 16 Complete Class Modules
Each module includes:
- Opening hook & core content
- Individual reflection prompts (4-5 questions)
- Online discussion prompts (2-3 peer questions)
- Action plan (5-7 specific activities with time estimates)
- Key takeaways (5-7 summary bullets)
- Resources (campus, tools, further reading)
- Supportive closing message

### ✅ Three Major Student Projects
Students create:
1. **Personal Purpose Statement** (Week 3) — Academic, career, personal goals
2. **Comprehensive Education Plan** (Week 12) — Semester-by-semester course mapping
3. **Career Goals Presentation** (Week 14) — Career direction & lifestyle expectations
4. **College Success Portfolio** (Week 16) — Capstone integrating all learning

### ✅ 8 Clear Learning Outcomes
All measurable and assessed through projects:
- Personal purpose statement creation
- Career goals articulation
- Education plan design
- Support system identification
- Community engagement
- Financial awareness
- Academic skills development
- Mental health & wellness maintenance

### ✅ Evidence-Based Content
- Learning science and growth mindset
- Time management and productivity strategies
- Note-taking systems research
- Career development models
- Financial literacy basics
- Wellness and resilience frameworks

### ✅ Inclusive Design
- Multiple formats and engagement pathways
- Acknowledges real student barriers (first-gen, working students, introverts, etc.)
- Flexible participation options
- Asynchronous delivery (24/7 access)
- Supportive tone (validation + empowerment)
